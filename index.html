About Ace of Braids

Welcome to Ace of Braids, Newcastle's premier destination for luxury braiding services. With over 6 years of experience, we specialize in creating stunning braid styles that celebrate your natural beauty.

Our mission is to provide exceptional service, premium quality, and a welcoming experience that makes every client feel confident and beautiful. We're passionate about our craft and committed to the highest standards of excellence.
